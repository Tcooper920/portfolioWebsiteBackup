Thomas Cooper

Thomas Cooper is a graduate from York College of Pennsylvania with several years of design experience. His primary focuses include both graphic design and front-end web development. While he excels in creating detail-oriented works, he also constructs clean, crisp, and sharp designs. His specialties include formatting type, responsive web design, illustration, developing identity systems, branding, logo design, print design, package design, digital advertising, and coding landing pages. Typography and white space are aspects of design that he finds intriguing. To Thomas, producing professional work means focusing on process, working the grid, conducting research, and seeking constructive criticism.
