Thomas Cooper

Hi! My name is Thomas. I am a graphic designer and web developer that is passionate about building brands both on and offline. As a firm believer of "less is more," I am constantly looking for ways to simplify convoluted designs without compromising communication.

While researching trends has its place, creating timeless designs is critical—and clean design often lends itself to memorability. When the context requires more detail, I won't hesitate to tap into my illustration skills. To me, producing professional work means focusing on process, working the grid, considering context, and seeking constructive criticism.
